About BHUMIPUTRA

Building Stronger Communities Through Comprehensive Empowerment

Founded with the vision of creating lasting positive change, BHUMIPUTRA is a pioneering organization dedicated to comprehensive community empowerment through commercial, economic, and social development initiatives.

Our integrated approach ensures sustainable growth by addressing the multifaceted needs of communities, creating pathways for economic independence, and fostering social cohesion that empowers individuals and families to thrive.

Our Products

Organic Fertilizer

Our premium organic fertilizer is crafted from naturally composted materials like plant residues, cow dung, and bio-waste.

High-Quality Seeds

Sourced from government-certified farms and thoroughly tested for high germination rates, our seeds are genetically pure and resistant to common pests and diseases.

Eco Irrigation System

Designed for maximum efficiency, our Eco Irrigation System utilizes drip and sprinkler technologies to ensure precise water delivery directly to plant roots, minimizing evaporation and runoff.
